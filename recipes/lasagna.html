Lasagna

Classic lasagna. This lasagna can feed a big family and can be frozen too!

Ingredients

For the meat sauce:

  • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1/2 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-oz) can tomato sauce
  • 3 oz tomato paste
  • 1 (14 oz) can crushed tomatos
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • Salt

To assemble the lasagna:

  • 1/2 lb dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 lbs mozzerlla cheese
  • 1/4 lb parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil on high heat.

  2. In a large skillet, heat 2 tsp of olive oil on medium high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until lightly browned. Remove the beef with a slotted spoon to a bowl. Drain almost all fat.

  3. Add the bell pepper and onions to a skillet and cook for 5 minutes. Then add the garlic and cook for 30 more seconds. Add the beef, reduce the heat to low and cook for another 5 minutes.

  4. Put the beef mixture into a medium-sized pot and add the tomatoes, tomato sauce and tomato paste. Add the seasonings. Bring the sauce to a simmer and then lower the heat. Cook for 15 to 45 minutes stirring often.

  5. Add the lasagna noodles to the boiling water and cook them according to the package. Drain and rinse. Spread some olive oil on a baking sheet and lay the noodles out turning them over so they get coated with olive oil.

  6. Heat the oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit.

  7. In a 9x13-in dish spread a cup of sauce over the bottom of the dish. Lay one layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce. Pour the remaining sauce over the noodles. Sprinkle mozzarella, half of the ricotta cheese, and half of the parmesan cheese on top. Add a second layer of noodles and top it with the sauce. Add half of the rest of the mozzarella, ricotta cheese, and parmesan cheese. Then add another layer of noodles and spread the rest of the sauce and top it with mozzarella cheese.

  8. Cover the pan with aluminum foil, lightly. Bake at 375 degrees fahrenheit for 45 minutes.

  9. Let cool and serve.
