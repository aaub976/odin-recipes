Strawberry Banana Smoothie

During a long warm day relax with this cool fresh strawberry banana smoothie! Made with four simple ingredients you'll be cooled off and relaxing in no time!

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 3 cups strawberries
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Steps

  1. Add all of the ingredients into the blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Enjoy!
